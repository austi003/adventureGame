Welcome young CodeNinja! Thanks for playing the CodeNinja Adventure Game!

CodeNinja is a text based RPG game that follows our hero, young CodeNinja. As the story progresses, you will be provided with various scenarios. As each scenario presents itself, you will be provided with a few possible alternative actions to choose from.
